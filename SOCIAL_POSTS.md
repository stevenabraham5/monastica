**Day 1: I built an app that asks "how are you right now?"**

Not a to-do list. Not a calendar. Not another productivity tool.

Tempo is a personal rhythm app. It tracks the shape of your whole life — sleep, movement, creative work, relationships, learning — not as tasks to complete, but as vessels that fill and empty over time.

It has an AI coach that doesn't just listen. It cross-examines. "Is that what actually happened, or what you think should have happened?" "What are you avoiding by focusing on this?"

Built in a single day from an empty repo:
- React Native (Expo SDK 55)
- Zustand for state
- Reanimated for 60fps animations
- Voice input on web and native
- Dutch minimalism design — Playfair Display, forest green, lots of white space

The core insight: you already know how to live well. You just lose the thread.

Looking for early testers. The app has a built-in dogfood mode — you can flag anything on any screen, suggest features, rate responses, and it all comes straight to me.

DM me or comment if you want to try it.
